📷 JamCam Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how JamCam ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our website.

JamCam does not collect any personal information. We do not require user accounts, registration, or personal data to use our service.

All traffic camera feeds displayed on JamCam are provided by the Singapore Land Transport Authority (LTA) DataMall. We do not store, record, or save any camera images.

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

4. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. You can also disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites.

6. Children's Privacy

JamCam does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
