📷 About JamCam

🎯 Our Mission: Help Singapore-Johor Bahru commuters make informed decisions about which checkpoint to use and when to cross.

What is JamCam?

JamCam provides live traffic camera feeds for the two main checkpoints between Singapore and Malaysia:

  • 🚗 Woodlands Causeway (both directions)
  • 🌉 Tuas Second Link

Features

  • Live camera feeds for 6 checkpoint locations
  • Traffic status badges (Heavy/Moderate/Light)
  • Wait time estimates based on historical patterns
  • Location maps for each checkpoint
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Auto-refresh every 2 minutes

How It Works

JamCam fetches live camera data from the LTA DataMall (Singapore Land Transport Authority) every 2 minutes. The data is then displayed in an easy-to-view format with traffic status badges and estimated wait times based on historical patterns (peak hours, weekends, holidays).

Data Source

All camera images are provided by LTA DataMall. We do not store any images. Maps are provided by OneMap Singapore.

Why We Built This

Commuting between Singapore and Johor Bahru can be unpredictable. Long queues at checkpoints can add hours to your journey. JamCam helps you check real-time traffic conditions before you cross, saving time and reducing stress.

Version History

  • v2.0 (March 2025) - Added dual maps, traffic status badges, wait time estimates
  • v1.0 (February 2025) - Initial launch with 4 checkpoint cameras

Disclaimer

Wait time estimates are based on historical patterns and may not reflect real-time conditions. Always check live camera feeds before making travel decisions.
