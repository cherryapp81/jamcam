About JamCam

What is JamCam?

JamCam provides live traffic camera feeds for SG-JB checkpoints including Woodlands Causeway and Tuas Second Link.

Data Source

All camera images are provided by LTA DataMall (Singapore Land Transport Authority).

Why We Built This

To help commuters check traffic conditions before crossing between Singapore and Johor Bahru.
